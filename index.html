Ground +

Ground Plus Engineering Consultants, established in 2021 in Al Dhaid, Sharjah, is a dynamic engineering firm specializing in comprehensive design and supervision services. With a focus on innovation, quality, and client satisfaction, we bring together a team of dedicated professionals to deliver exceptional engineering solutions for projects of all scales and complexities.

Our primary goal is our presence in the domestic market in designing, managing and building fields. At GroundPlus Engineering Consultants, our mission is to shape our client's vision into reality. We proudly deliver exceptional design ideas and solutions for our client's through the innovative blending of human needs and urban developments.

Planning

Ground Plus Engineering Consultants offers comprehensive planning services that encompass everything from conceptualization to feasibility studies and preliminary design. We collaborate closely with our clients to gain a deep understanding of their goals and requirements, conducting thorough assessments and analyses to develop strategic plans that serve as the foundation for successful project execution.

Supervision

Ground Plus Engineering Consultants offers supervision services aimed at ensuring the smooth and efficient execution of projects, from inception to completion. We provide comprehensive oversight and management to ensure that projects are delivered on time, within budget, and to the highest quality standards.

Licensing & Approval Services

Ground Plus Engineering Consultants offers approval services that streamline the regulatory approval process, ensuring compliance with local regulations and requirements. We navigate the complex approval process on behalf of our clients, facilitating timely approvals and permits to keep projects on track.
